## 📖 The Premise

**What if the Ilinden Uprising succeeded — not on the battlefield, but in the halls of power?**

In 1903, the Ilinden Uprising failed militarily. But in this timeline, IMRO's leadership survived intact. Goce Delchev. Jane Sandanski. Nikola Karev. Over the next five years, they negotiated unprecedented autonomy from the crumbling Ottoman Empire. In 1912, they seized their moment — the **Solun Uprising** secured the Aegean port that would become the republic's capital.

Bulgaria invaded twice — 1913 and 1915. Both times, Macedonia held. Both times, Greece watched.

Now it is 1936. The old revolutionaries are fading. The interim president, **Metodija Čento**, presides over a deadlocked parliament. Ten paths. Four ideologies. One question:

**Who will lead Macedonia into the gathering storm?**

## 🎯 Features

### 🌳 Five Interconnected Focus Trees (~80+ Focuses)

| Tree | Theme | Highlights |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Political** | *"The Delchev Succession"* | 10 candidate paths across 4 ideologies. Democratic primary, monarchist restoration, military junta, people's army, or iron guard |
| **Military** | *"The Army of the Lion"* | Shared trunk → mutually exclusive Aleksandrov (defensive) vs. Apostolski (offensive) doctrines → mountain, air, and naval sub-branches |
| **Economic** | *"The Balkan Workshop"* | Military & civilian industry, Vardar infrastructure, tobacco empire (TKP), and the opium trade (Alkaloid) |
| **Diplomatic** | *"Macedonia Among Nations"* | Balkan League faction, Great Power alignment (4 mutually exclusive paths), ideology-specific irredentism methods |
| **Cultural** | *"Heirs of Alexander"* | Vergina heritage, festival of the sun, White Tower restoration, rakija spirits, Macedonian Alexander Airways |

### 🌺 Opium Trade Empire

Macedonia leverages poppy exports into a shadow empire of influence. A custom scripted GUI tracks:
- Per-nation dependency stages (0–4), from casual use to state-level addiction
- Aggregate leverage score across the Balkans
- Timed influence missions and diplomatic pressure
- Top-5 trade partner bar visualisation

Target Greece to compromise their officer corps. Supply Balkan neighbours to build dependency. Sell pharmaceuticals to the Great Powers — and hope they never connect the dots.

### 🏗️ Dynamic National Spirits

10+ national spirits that evolve throughout a playthrough — added, upgraded, downgraded, or removed by focuses, events, and decisions. No static modifiers sitting dead in the idea panel.

- `MAC_founder_watches` — Delchev's presence fades when succession resolves
- `MAC_old_guard_reform` — Resolved by military doctrine choice
- `MAC_delcevism` — Survives or falls depending on ideological path
- `MAC_poppy` — Expands through the opium economic branch

### 🗺️ Custom Map

15+ reworked states across the Balkans:
- **Eastern Rumelia** (1089) — new releasable state with tricolor flag
- **Edirne** (341), **Burgas** (211), **Istanbul** (797) — border refinements
- **Pirin** (1082), **East Aegean** (1083), **Western Aegean** (1085), **Pogradec** (1087), **Rhodopes** (1088) — Macedonian-region states
- Constantinople/Istanbul dynamic province naming by owner

### 📊 Scripted GUI

- **Parliament diagram** — visualise political composition across 5 ideological columns
- **Pie chart** — economic data visualisation
- **Trade tracker** — floating toggle, top-5 partner bars, commodity counts

## 🔧 Installation

1. **Subscribe on Steam** (recommended) or clone this repository
2. If installing manually, place the folder into your HOI4 mod directory:

| Platform | Path |
|----------|------|
| **Windows** | `Documents\Paradox Interactive\Hearts of Iron IV\mod\` |
| **Linux** | `~/.local/share/Paradox Interactive/Hearts of Iron IV/mod/` |
| **Mac** | `~/Documents/Paradox Interactive/Hearts of Iron IV/mod/` |

3. Enable **"Shadows Over The Balkans — New Sun of Liberty"** in the HOI4 launcher

> **Requires:** Hearts of Iron IV **v1.19*** or later. No DLC required *(some features enhanced with DLCs)*.
